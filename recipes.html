Recipe 1: Fried Chicken

Definitely one of my favorites!

List of Ingredients:

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 2 tablespoons garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 3 teaspoons pepper, divided
  • 2-1/2 teaspoons poultry seasoning
  • 2 large Nellie’s Free Range Eggs
  • 1-1/2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 broiler/fryer chickens (3-1/2 to 4 pounds each), cut up
  • Oil for deep-fat frying

Steps:

  1. In a large resealable plastic bag, combine 2-2/3 cups flour, garlic salt, paprika, 2-1/2 teaspoons pepper and poultry seasoning.
  2. In a shallow bowl, beat eggs and water; add salt and the remaining flour and pepper.
  3. Dip chicken in egg mixture, then place in the bag, a few pieces at a time.
  4. Seal bag and shake to coat.
  5. In a deep-fat fryer, heat oil to 375°.
  6. Fry chicken, several pieces at a time, for 5-6 minutes on each side or until golden brown and juices run clear.
  7. Drain on paper towels.
